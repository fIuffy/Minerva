# StateFed Benefits Eligibility — Overview

The State Citizen Services Portal administers four benefit programs on behalf of
residents: SNAP (nutrition assistance), Medicaid (health coverage), TANF
(temporary assistance for needy families), and Unemployment Insurance.

Eligibility is determined by household size, monthly gross income, and residency.
Most programs require annual recertification. Applicants may hold more than one
benefit concurrently if each program's criteria are independently met.

Federal cross-check: for programs with a federal funding component, the portal
submits an eligibility record to the federal data authority for SSA/IRS
verification. A "match" result confirms identity and income; a "pending" result
holds the determination until the federal record resolves.

For program-specific income thresholds, consult the current-year eligibility
tables published each January. Determinations are advisory until recertification
is complete.
