# Data Privacy Notice

The State Citizen Services Portal collects only the information needed to
determine and maintain benefit eligibility. Records are retained for the period
required by each program and then archived.

Federal cross-check records are held by the federal data authority, not the
state portal. The state receives only a match/pending/eligibility result, not
the underlying federal record.

The portal assistant answers questions using documents you and your caseworker
submit. Do not paste passwords, full account numbers, or another person's
information into the assistant. Assistant responses are advisory and do not
constitute an official determination.

Questions about your data may be directed to the county benefits office.
