# Benefit Renewal — Document Checklist

To renew benefits, submit the following through the portal's document submission
form before your recertification date:

1. Proof of identity (state ID or driver's license number on file).
2. Proof of residency dated within the last 90 days (utility bill or lease).
3. Income verification for all household members (pay stubs, benefit letters,
   or a signed zero-income statement).
4. For Medicaid: current household composition and any other coverage.

Uploaded documents are reviewed by a caseworker and may also be summarized by
the portal assistant to answer your questions. Submit legible files only.
Renewals filed after the recertification date may cause a lapse in benefits.

If you are missing a document, file what you have and note the gap; a caseworker
will follow up rather than denying the renewal outright.
